Bachata

Are you always Dancing through life? You can’t stop the beat? Well come along to Broadway and Step in time to fun, jazzy and energetic routines discovering different genres and eras. Musical Theatre is all about character so the class will also help with performing and story-telling! No need to be shy and prepared to get silly! Don’t worry I won’t make you sing but of course do if you’re feeling it! Every level is welcome, whether you are a musical theatre connoisseur or just starting out! Come along for a taste of FAME in every class. Just bring your smiles and personalities and get ready to Razzle Dazzle!

Tutor: Chloe Mcmillan

Irish

Irish beginners: If you've never done Irish dancing or are just starting out, come along and give it a go! No experience necessary, just a willingness to try new things! It's a great work out, great music and great fun!

Irish intermediate/advanced: No specific experience required, we'll work to all levels; but if you have done some Irish dancing before, or are currently an Irish dancer, come along and try this class! It's a great work out, great music and great fun!

Tango Argentino

if you are thinking about a weird stiff couple dancing with roses in their mouths, this is not Tango Argentino..! This dance is about being able to improvise together, interpreting the music with elegance, being aware of your body and your partner. It ́s very much like learning a language: we teach you its basics, we explain the words and the possible dynamics, but it ́s up to you to combine those elements to express yourself. Anybody can dance Tango, whatever their dance skills and age: if you ́ve never danced anything before, Tango Argentino is a great way to start, as it does not require any physical talent; if you ́re a dancer, Tango will enhance your improvisation skills; if you ́re a couple dancer, it will improve your communication skills dramatically. Basically there is no reason for not dancing Tango Argentino! Come and join us :D

Tutor: Chloe Mcmillan
