Bachata

If you mean it seriously with salsa, you just can't ignore bachata as every Latin social plays both. Why? Well, can you imagine yourself dancing highly energetic salsa for 3 hours in a row? And that is why every Latin DJ gives you a chance to slow down and get (just a bit 😉 ) closer for a couple of times with this romantic Dominican dance. But don't be mistaken by the slower pace - with its fancy footwork and sensual moves bachata can get as tricky as salsa!

Capoeira

Capoeira is a Brazilian martial art with its roots originating in Angola and the Congo. It combines elements of dance, acrobatics and music. The main element of capoeira is the Roda is a circle formed by capoeiristas and capoeira musical instruments.

The style entails complex moves, using power, speed and leverage for a wide variety of kicks, spins and technical moves. It is suitable for everyone, no matter if you are a beginner or have previous experience. In capoeira we try to learn from each other so we tend to mix our classes with all levels to create a fun atmosphere for everyone!

Commercial & Booty

Commercial is a derivation of hip hop dance with some elements from other styles such as jazz. Unlike old school hip hop, this style was not born on the streets but in commercial dance studios. This is the kind of “hip hop” you see in many modern music videos and concerts.

In this particular class, the commercial style will be fusioned with moves from dancehall and twerk - especially the different booty shakes. This class offers you a super sexy, feminine and entertaining blend of different dance styles!

Dancehall

Dancehall originated in Jamaica in late 1970s - 1980s and it is still evolving nowadays. There are new steps being created every day and more and more people are finding their way to try this very positive and energetic (life)style.

No matter if you are a complete beginner or an intermediate - come along and give dancehall a go! If you like Jamaican music, style and vibes but also a good workout, this class is perfect for you!

There will be choreos, jams and a lot of positive energy. See yuh dere!

Indian Classical & Folk

Indian classical dance is an umbrella term for seven main ancient dance forms of India. This dance group focuses on the two most popular forms known as Bharatanatyam and Kathak. These dances are an intricate combination of beautiful hand gestures, leg work and expressions that dancers use together to convey a story.

We also teach Indian folk dancing which is a vibrant and energetic form of Indian dance performed in every joyful occasions. In this dance class we will focus on teaching the best of all these three forms to give you a flavour of the vibrancy, elegance and beauty that these dance forms have to offer.

Reggaeton

Perreo, more commonly known as Reggaeton like the music it is performed on, is a popular form of dance which can often be seen in Latin music videos and night clubs. Originally from Puerto Rico, and with Jamaican, Latin American, and Hip Hop influences, Reggaeton is a sexy, energetic and passionate dance. If you want to build your confidence, or just let loose for an hour, this all levels class is a good choice! Reggaeton can be a single or couple dance, and everyone is welcome to join. Hope to see you there!

Salsa LA

The style of Salsa which is known as LA is well-known for its flashy moves and danced in a linear “cross body” fashion. This partnered dance is VERY social, so get ready to meet and dance with a variety of people from all over the world.

The beginner’s class will introduce the basics to you along with some fun moves. After just one class you’ll be able to get on the dance floor enjoying the music and interacting with the dance community at our Latin socials. As you improve we’ll use your basics to introduce somewhat more challenging figures and footwork while continuing to expose you to the diversity of music that you can dance to!

Streetdance

Streetdance means many different things for different people and it is usually done right with a group of people coming together and jamming. We do everything from locking to old school to whipping and nae-naeing. You are also welcome and encouraged to develop your own street-style during the classes and simply just express yourself freely.

Come and join us and bring a few friends while you’re at it. See you in class! 😀

Tango Argentino

If you are thinking about a stiff couple dancing with roses in their mouths, this is not Tango Argentino! This dance is about being able to improvise together, interpreting the music with elegance, being aware of your body and your partner.

Anybody can dance Tango, whatever their dance skills: if you´ve never danced anything before, Tango Argentino is a great way to start, as it does not require any physical talent; if you´re a dancer, Tango will enhance your improvisation skills; if you´re a couple dancer, it will improve your communication skills dramatically. Basically there is no reason for not dancing Tango Argentino! Come and join us :D

Tap

Tap dance encompasses a variety of styles and is a great way to keep fit and improve balance and co-ordination. It makes you feel you are creating music, or adding to the song you are dancing to.

The class is intermediate so some experience is preferred. However if you’re enthusiastic then come along and try it out. Tap is better with tap shoes but don’t worry if you don’t have just wear some shoes eg trainers, character shoes to avoid injury!

Voguing

Voguing evolved into a dance style in New York, where dancers battled with poses that were copied from the fashion magazine Vogue. Voguing still involves poses and typically Voguing involves geometric arm movements and is elegant and feminine.

Classes focus mainly on Vogue Fem which a feminine Voguing style where it is softer and smoother than in other Voguing styles. Key techniques (posing, arm movements, runway walks) are covered in the classes. Traditionally, Voguing is freestyled in dance battles and the classes also explore Voguing freestyle techniques in addition to Voguing choreographies.
